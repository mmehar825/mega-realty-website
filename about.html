About Mega Realty

A Philadelphia real estate firm built on integrity, expertise, and a commitment to helping our clients and agents succeed.

Our Story

Built on Relationships, Driven by Results

At Mega Realty, you're getting more than just a real estate service — you're getting a partner dedicated to achieving your goals. Our team's profound market knowledge ensures unparalleled service across buying, selling, leasing, or managing properties.

We pride ourselves on creating personalized solutions that fit your unique needs, making the complex real estate process seamless and stress-free. With a proven track record of success and a host of satisfied clients, our results speak for themselves.

We also believe that great agents deserve a great brokerage. That's why we offer a 100% commission model with low flat fees — giving our agents the freedom and support they need to build thriving businesses.
