Common Questions

Do I need experience to join?

Not at all. The free course is designed for complete beginners. Whether you've never done a deal or you're looking to scale, there's value here for you.

Is the deal flow only in Philadelphia?

No — we cover all of Pennsylvania through Mega Realty's statewide agent network. You'll see deals from Philly, the suburbs, Lehigh Valley, Poconos, Central PA, and Western PA.

What does co-wholesaling with Mega agents mean?

Inner Circle members can partner (JV) with Mega Realty agents on assignments. You bring the deal, our agents bring the buyers — and you split the assignment fee. It's the fastest way to close when you're building your own buyers list.

Can I cancel the Inner Circle anytime?

Yes — cancel anytime with no penalties. You'll keep access to the free community and course, you just lose the Inner Circle benefits.

Inner Circle members who decide to get licensed can join Mega Realty with our first-year-free promotion and get priority mentorship from our top-producing agents. Many wholesalers eventually get licensed to expand their business — we make that transition seamless.
