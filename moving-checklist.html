Moving Checklist

A week-by-week guide to make sure nothing falls through the cracks during your move.

8 Weeks Before Moving Day

✓
Create a moving budget — include movers, supplies, deposits, and travel costs
✓
Research and get quotes from at least 3 moving companies
✓
Start decluttering — donate, sell, or discard items you won't need
✓
Create a folder for important documents (lease, mortgage docs, IDs)
✓
If you have kids, research schools and request records transfer

6 Weeks Before

✓
Book your moving company and confirm the date
✓
Start collecting packing supplies — boxes, tape, bubble wrap, markers
✓
Notify your landlord if you're renting (check lease for notice requirements)
✓
Start packing non-essential items — seasonal clothes, books, decor
✓
Arrange for medical records, prescriptions, and vet records to transfer

4 Weeks Before

✓
Submit a change of address with USPS
✓
Update your address with banks, insurance, subscriptions, and employers
✓
Transfer or set up utilities at your new home (electric, gas, water, internet)
✓
Continue packing room by room — label every box clearly
✓
Arrange for childcare or pet care on moving day

2 Weeks Before

✓
Confirm moving company details — arrival time, address, special instructions
✓
Clean out refrigerator and plan to use up perishable food
✓
Return borrowed items and collect anything you've lent out
✓
Prepare a "first night" box — toiletries, change of clothes, medications, chargers, snacks
✓
Take photos of electronics setups before disconnecting

Moving Week

✓
Finish packing everything except essentials
✓
Defrost freezer and clean all appliances
✓
Do a final walkthrough — check every closet, drawer, and cabinet
✓
Keep important documents, valuables, and medications with you (not on the truck)
✓
Say goodbye to neighbors and leave keys/garage openers as needed

Moving Day

✓
Be present when movers arrive — walk through the plan with the crew lead
✓
Do a final sweep of every room before leaving
✓
Check that all utilities are off at the old place and on at the new one
✓
Review and sign inventory and condition sheets with movers
✓
Inspect the new home before unloading — note any issues

After You're In

✓
Update your driver's license and vehicle registration (Pennsylvania requires this within 60 days)
✓
Register to vote at your new address
✓
Find new doctors, dentists, and veterinarians
✓
Change locks and reprogram garage door openers
✓
Meet your neighbors — you'd be surprised how much it helps
