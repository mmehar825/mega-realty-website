Mega Realty Launches Investor Network Community

December 20, 2024 · 3 min read
← Back to Blog

Building Something New

At Mega Realty, we've always believed that real estate is about more than just transactions. It's about building networks, sharing knowledge, and creating opportunities for everyone involved — agents, investors, and the communities we serve. Today, we're excited to announce the launch of the Mega Investor Network, a community designed specifically for real estate investors and wholesalers across Pennsylvania.

Why We Built This

We noticed a gap in the market. There are plenty of national real estate investing communities and courses out there, but very few focused specifically on Pennsylvania. The laws are different here. The markets are different. The opportunities are different. A wholesaling strategy that works in Texas doesn't necessarily translate to Philadelphia or the Lehigh Valley.

We wanted to create a space where PA-focused investors could learn from people who actually operate in these markets, access real deals (not theoretical ones), and build a network that leads to closed transactions.

What's Included — Free

Every member gets access to our complete wholesaling course covering everything from finding motivated sellers to closing your first assignment. The course is built specifically around Pennsylvania law and markets. You also get access to the community discussion feed where investors share wins, ask questions, and network. We publish weekly PA market updates and maintain a resource library of scripts, checklists, and contract templates.

And yes — it's actually free. No credit card required, no "free trial" that converts to a charge. We believe in providing real value upfront.

The Inner Circle

For investors who want to go deeper, our Inner Circle membership ($149/month) provides access to exclusive live deal flow from Mega Realty's agent network across Pennsylvania. Members can co-wholesale deals with our agents — you bring the deal, our network provides the buyers, and you split the assignment fee. You also get weekly live deal review calls where experienced investors look at your numbers before you commit, plus a private chat and direct access to Mega's buyer network.

The Bigger Vision

The Mega Investor Network isn't just a community — it's a pipeline. Wholesalers who learn and grow with us often decide to get licensed, and when they do, Mega Realty is there with our 100% commission model and first-year-free promotion. We're building a complete ecosystem: learn to invest, access deals, grow your skills, and eventually build a full real estate career if that's what you want.

Whether you're a complete beginner curious about wholesaling or an experienced investor looking for PA-specific deal flow, there's a place for you in the Mega Investor Network.
