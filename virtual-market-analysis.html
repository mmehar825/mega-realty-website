What Is a Comparative Market Analysis?

A CMA is a professional evaluation of your property's value based on real market data. Unlike automated online estimates (like Zestimates), a CMA is prepared by a licensed agent who considers factors that algorithms miss — your home's condition, upgrades, lot characteristics, street appeal, and local micro-market trends.

✓

✓

✓
Estimated net proceeds after selling costs
✓
Personalized recommendations for maximizing value

Why Online Estimates Fall Short

Automated home value tools are useful as a starting point, but they frequently miss the mark by 5–15% or more. They can't account for recent renovations, neighborhood nuances, or the condition of your property compared to comparable homes. A professional CMA gives you a realistic, data-backed valuation you can actually rely on.
